FenomenStar Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

English

FenomenStar is a short-video social platform. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, password hash, profile fields.
  • Content data: uploaded videos, thumbnails, comments, likes, profile media.
  • Technical data: device/app logs, IP, crash and performance diagnostics.
  • Session/security data: authentication tokens and refresh sessions.

2. Why We Process Data

  • To provide login, profile, upload, feed, search, and social interactions.
  • To secure the app, prevent abuse/fraud, and enforce platform rules.
  • To moderate content and comply with store/platform requirements.
  • To improve reliability, stability, and user experience.

3. Storage and Infrastructure

  • Authentication and database services may be provided by Supabase/PostgreSQL.
  • Video files may be stored on Cloudflare R2 (or compatible object storage).
  • Caching/queue services may be provided by Redis-compatible infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers required to run the app (hosting, storage, analytics, moderation, security), and only to the extent necessary.

5. Retention

We keep personal data while your account is active or as required for legal/security reasons. You can request account/content deletion via the contact address below.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards (access control, token-based auth, rate limiting, logging, and monitoring). No method is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. Contact us to submit requests.

8. Contact

Email: thefenomenstar@gmail.com

Turkce

FenomenStar, kisa video iceriklerinin olusturuldugu ve paylasildigi bir sosyal platformdur. Bu metin, hangi verileri topladigimizi, neden isledigimizi ve nasil korudugumuzu aciklar.

1. Toplanan Veriler

  • Hesap verileri: ad, e-posta, sifre hash bilgisi, profil alanlari.
  • Icerik verileri: yuklenen videolar, thumbnail, yorum, begeni ve profil medyasi.
  • Teknik veriler: cihaz/uygulama loglari, IP, hata ve performans kayitlari.
  • Oturum guvenligi verileri: erisim tokenlari ve refresh oturumlari.

2. Isleme Amaclari

  • Giris, profil, yukleme, feed, arama ve etkilesim ozelliklerini sunmak.
  • Kotuye kullanim/fraud onlemek ve platform guvenligini saglamak.
  • Icerik moderasyonu yapmak ve uygulama magazasi gereksinimlerine uymak.
  • Uygulamanin stabilitesini ve deneyimini iyilestirmek.

3. Altyapi ve Saklama

  • Kimlik dogrulama ve veritabani servisleri Supabase/PostgreSQL uzerinden saglanabilir.
  • Video dosyalari Cloudflare R2 (veya uyumlu obje depolama) uzerinde tutulabilir.
  • Kuyruk/cache islemleri Redis uyumlu servislerle yonetilebilir.

4. Veri Paylasimi

Kisisel veriler satilmaz. Veriler yalnizca hizmetin calismasi icin gerekli altyapi saglayicilari ile, gerekli oldugu kadar paylasilir.

5. Saklama Sureleri

Veriler, hesap aktif oldugu surece veya hukuki/guvenlik gereklilikleri kapsaminda saklanir. Silme talepleri icin asagidaki iletisim adresi kullanilabilir.

6. Guvenlik

Erisim kontrolu, token tabanli kimlik dogrulama, rate limit ve loglama gibi makul onlemler uygulanir. Hicbir sistemin %100 guvenli olmadigi unutulmamalidir.

7. Haklariniz

Bulundugunuz bolgeye gore erisim, duzeltme, silme veya islemeyi kisitlama taleplerinde bulunabilirsiniz.

8. Iletisim

E-posta: thefenomenstar@gmail.com
