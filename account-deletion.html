FenomenStar Account Deletion

This page explains how FenomenStar users can request account deletion.

English

If you want to delete your FenomenStar account, send an email to thefenomenstar@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.

Steps

  1. Use the email address associated with your FenomenStar account.
  2. Send an email with the subject line Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include your account email and, if available, your display name.
  4. We may request basic verification to prevent unauthorized deletion requests.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile data, including name, email, bio, and profile fields.
  • Uploaded content associated with your account, subject to operational and legal limits.
  • Associated social data such as likes, comments, follows, and notifications where applicable.

What may be retained for a limited period

  • Security, fraud-prevention, and compliance logs.
  • Backup copies retained for a limited operational recovery period.
  • Records required by law or needed to resolve abuse, fraud, or policy violations.

Retained data is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for legal, security, and operational purposes.

Contact

Email: thefenomenstar@gmail.com

Turkce

FenomenStar hesabinizi silmek istiyorsaniz, hesabinizla baglantili e-posta adresinden thefenomenstar@gmail.com adresine e-posta gonderin.

Izlenecek Adimlar

  1. FenomenStar hesabinizla iliskili e-posta adresini kullanin.
  2. Konu satirina Hesap Silme Talebi yazin.
  3. Hesap e-postanizi ve varsa gorunen adinizi belirtin.
  4. Yetkisiz talepleri onlemek icin temel dogrulama istenebilir.

Silinecek Veriler

  • Ad, e-posta, bio ve profil alanlari dahil hesap verileri.
  • Hukuki ve operasyonel sinirlar dahilinde hesaba bagli yuklenmis icerikler.
  • Uygun oldugu olcude begeni, yorum, takip ve bildirim gibi iliskili sosyal veriler.

Sinirli Sureyle Saklanabilecek Veriler

  • Guvenlik, kotuye kullanim onleme ve uyumluluk kayitlari.
  • Sinirli operasyonel yedekleme sureleri boyunca tutulan kopyalar.
  • Yasal yukumlulukler veya kotuye kullanim/fraud incelemeleri icin gerekli kayitlar.

Saklanan veriler yalnizca yasal, guvenlik ve operasyonel gereklilikler boyunca tutulur.
